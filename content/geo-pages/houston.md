## Immediate steps

1. Move to safety and call 911 if anyone is injured.
2. Do not admit fault — document facts only.
3. Photograph all vehicles, plates, and road conditions.
4. Exchange insurance and registration information.
5. Collect witness names and phone numbers.
6. Seek medical care within 24 hours.
7. Notify your insurer — decline recorded statements first.
8. Preserve dashcam or security video immediately.

## Critical claim steps

1. Medical evaluation within 24 hours — insurers use treatment gaps against you.
2. Obtain the police / Texas crash report for Harris County.
3. Log every adjuster contact with date and name.
4. Avoid social media posts about the crash.
5. Track lost wages and medical mileage.
6. Review UM/UIM and MedPay on your Texas policy.
7. Do not accept the first settlement offer.
8. Consult a Texas attorney before signing releases.

2. Recorded statements designed to minimize injury
3. Delay then pressure to sign
4. Disputing whiplash without imaging
5. Blaming pre-existing conditions
