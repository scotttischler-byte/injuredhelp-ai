## Immediate steps

1. Move to safety and call 911 for injuries.
2. Document the scene with photos and video.
3. Exchange insurance information with all drivers.
4. Identify witnesses before they leave.
5. Seek same-day medical evaluation if possible.
6. Request police crash report number.
7. Notify insurer without a recorded statement.
8. Save all medical bills and visit summaries.
