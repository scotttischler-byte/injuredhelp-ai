## Immediate steps

1. Ensure safety on I-35W or Loop 820 — use hazards.
2. Call 911 for injuries and police report.
3. Document all vehicles and license plates.
4. Note road construction or weather conditions.

6. Exchange insurance with all parties.
7. Decline recorded insurer statements initially.
8. Save tow, rental, and medical receipts.
