## Immediate steps

1. Call 911 and request police report.
2. Photograph vehicles, injuries, and traffic controls.
3. Do not admit fault at the scene.
4. Collect witness and officer badge information.
5. Seek trauma-capable care if seriously injured.
6. Notify insurance — no recorded statement yet.
7. Preserve black box / commercial data if truck involved.
8. Contact free attorney matching if injuries are significant.
