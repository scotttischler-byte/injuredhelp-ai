## Immediate steps

1. Move to safety and call 911 if anyone is injured.
2. Do not admit fault — stick to facts.
3. Photograph vehicles, plates, injuries, and road conditions.
4. Exchange insurance and registration with all drivers.
5. Collect witness names and phone numbers.
6. Seek medical care within 24 hours.
7. Notify your insurer — decline recorded statements first.
8. Preserve dashcam, Ring, or business security video.
