## Immediate steps

1. Move to safety and call 911 if anyone is injured.
2. Do not admit fault — document facts only.
3. Photograph vehicles, plates, injuries, and conditions in Atlanta.
4. Exchange insurance and registration with all drivers.
5. Collect witness names and phone numbers.
6. Seek medical care within 24 hours.
7. Notify your insurer — decline recorded statements first.
8. Preserve dashcam, traffic camera, or business security video.

1. Get medical evaluation within 24 hours — insurers in Atlanta dispute treatment gaps.
2. Obtain the police / crash report for your Atlanta jurisdiction.
3. Log every adjuster contact with dates and names.
4. Do not post about the crash on social media.
5. Track lost wages; Georgia SOL is typically 2 years — confirm with counsel.
6. Review UM/UIM coverage on your policy.
