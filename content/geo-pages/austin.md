## Immediate steps

1. Move off I-35 or MoPac if safe after crash.
2. Call 911 — Austin Police or DPS depending on location.
3. Photograph scene including construction signage.
4. Collect rideshare or delivery app trip details if applicable.
5. Seek care at Dell Seton or urgent care promptly.
6. Document pedestrian or cyclist involvement.
7. Notify insurer in writing when possible.
8. Preserve video from CapMetro or business cameras.
