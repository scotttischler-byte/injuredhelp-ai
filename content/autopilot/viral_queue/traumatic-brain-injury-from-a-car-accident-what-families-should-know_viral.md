## Reddit (helpful comment — follow sub rules)

TBI claims after car accidents are uniquely complicated because symptoms often don't appear immediately. Headaches, memory issues, mood changes, and cognitive problems can emerge days or weeks after the crash — which means many families don't connect them to the accident until later. Always get a full neurological evaluation documented as early as possible, even if the ER initially cleared your loved one.

On the legal side, most states have a 2-year statute of limitations for personal injury claims, but some are shorter. Insurance companies know this and sometimes intentionally slow-walk communications hoping families miss the window. Do not accept any settlement offer before speaking with an attorney — early offers almost never account for long-term care, lost income, or ongoing rehabilitation costs.
