## Reddit (helpful comment — follow sub rules)

Tractor-trailer accidents in Texas are legally very different from standard car crashes. These collisions fall under FMCSA federal regulations, and liability can extend beyond just the driver to include the trucking company, cargo shipper, and maintenance contractors. Identifying all liable parties early is critical because each may have separate insurance policies and legal exposure.

One of the most urgent priorities is evidence preservation. Commercial trucks carry electronic logging devices (ELDs) and event data recorders (black boxes) that capture speed, braking, and hours of service data. Trucking companies are not required to hold this data indefinitely, and without a legal preservation letter sent quickly, it can be overwritten or lost. An experienced truck accident attorney can send that letter within hours.

If you're in this situation, avoid giving recorded statements to any insurance adjuster before speaking with an attorney—trucking company insurers move fast and their goal is minimizing your claim.
