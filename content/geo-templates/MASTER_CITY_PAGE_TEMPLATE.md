## Information island 1 — Immediate steps after a crash in [City]

After a collision in [City], act quickly to protect your health and claim. These steps are **educational only**:

1. Move to a safe location and call **911** if anyone is injured.
2. Do **not** admit fault — document facts only.
3. Photograph vehicles, plates, injuries, road conditions, and traffic signals.
4. Exchange insurance and registration with all drivers.
5. Collect witness names and phone numbers.
6. Seek medical care within **24 hours** (even if pain feels minor).
7. Notify your insurer — **decline recorded statements** until you understand your rights.
8. Preserve dashcam, Ring, business security, or traffic camera footage.
