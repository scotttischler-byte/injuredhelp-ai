## Common injuries

- Road rash and scarring  
- Fractures and foot/ankle injuries  
- Traumatic brain injury (even with helmets)  
- Spinal injuries  

## Bias at the scene

Police and insurers may assume the rider was at fault. Witnesses, helmet cam video, and skid marks matter.

## Legal steps

1. Get medical care and keep all records  
2. Preserve gear and bike damage photos  
3. Avoid recorded statements to the other insurer  
4. Review UM/UIM on your motorcycle policy  

## Helmet and lane-splitting laws

Rules vary by state and can affect comparative fault arguments.

- **Quick cash offers** before MRI results or specialist referrals return
