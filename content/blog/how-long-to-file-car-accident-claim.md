## Statutes of limitations

Most states give you **two to three years** to file a personal injury lawsuit after a car accident, but rules differ for minors, government vehicles, and uninsured claims.

## Why act sooner

Evidence fades: witnesses move, camera footage gets deleted, and injuries can worsen over weeks.
