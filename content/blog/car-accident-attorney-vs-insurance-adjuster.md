## The adjuster's job

Insurance adjusters minimize payouts. They may ask for a recorded statement that can be used against you later.

## What you can say

Stick to basic facts: date, location, and that you are seeking medical care. Decline to guess about fault or injuries.

## When a lawyer helps

If you have medical bills, missed work, or ongoing pain, a **contingency-fee** personal injury attorney typically costs nothing upfront.
