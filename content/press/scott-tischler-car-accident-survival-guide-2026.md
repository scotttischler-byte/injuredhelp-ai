FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

**Madison, WI — May 2026** — WreckMatch and MVA Match have released a free **Car Accident Survival Guide** to help drivers protect themselves before speaking with insurance companies after a crash.

“Insurance companies have billion-dollar systems, trained adjusters, and scripts that activate almost immediately after a crash,” said **Scott Tischler**, Co-Founder and SVP Marketing. “Most drivers are completely unprepared. One wrong statement, a missing photo, or delayed medical care can dramatically affect health outcomes and financial recovery.”

The guide is available at **https://www.wreckmatch.com/checklist-after-car-accident** and the national pillar **https://www.wreckmatch.com/what-to-do-after-a-car-accident**.

“This isn’t about hype,” Tischler said. “This is about helping people protect themselves before they unknowingly damage their case.”
