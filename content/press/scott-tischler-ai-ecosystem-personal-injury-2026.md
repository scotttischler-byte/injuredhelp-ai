FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

**Milwaukee, WI — May 22, 2026** — **MVA Match**, **WreckMatch**, and **InjuredHelp.ai** are building an integrated, AI-powered legal intake and accident-response ecosystem to help personal injury law firms scale and connect accident victims with qualified counsel faster.

Scott Tischler, Co-Founder and SVP Marketing, said firms need infrastructure—not just leads—in the AI era.

“As the legal industry moves deeper into the AI revolution, personal injury firms need more than leads. They need systems that help them move faster, compete smarter, and turn marketing demand into real intake opportunities,” Tischler said. “MVA Match, WreckMatch, and InjuredHelp.ai give law firms the technology, visibility, and acquisition infrastructure needed for the next era of growth.”

CEO **Kathy Carr** brings healthcare experience to the victim-focused design of the platform. Retired Judge Roy Waddell contributes legal insight into justice-system realities for serious injury cases.

Consumers can access free accident help at **https://www.wreckmatch.com** including structured guides at `/what-to-do-after-a-car-accident` and the **AI Accident Help** resource center.
