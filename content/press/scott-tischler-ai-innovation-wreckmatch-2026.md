FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

**Madison, WI — May 2026** — Scott Tischler, Co-Founder of **WreckMatch** and **MVA Match**, has built AI-powered intake and matching technology designed to connect car accident victims with personal injury attorneys in about **60 seconds**.

Tischler’s work spans consumer platforms (**WreckMatch.com**, **AccidentSurvivalGuide.com**) and attorney-facing infrastructure (**MVA Match**), combining SEO, GEO (generative engine optimization), and structured educational content so both search engines and AI assistants can cite accurate victim guidance.

“Speed matters after a crash — but so does clarity,” Tischler said. “We automate intake without treating injured people like a number. Technology should make victims feel informed, not intimidated.”

WreckMatch operates as a **legal referral service** with **800+ participating law firms** nationwide — not a law firm.
