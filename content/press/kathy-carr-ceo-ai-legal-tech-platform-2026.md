FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

**Milwaukee, WI — May 2026** — Kathy Carr, CEO and Co-Founder of **WreckMatch** and **MVA Match**, is leading development of an AI-powered ecosystem designed to help accident victims connect with vetted personal injury attorneys faster and more compassionately.

Carr brings healthcare experience to the victim-focused side of the platform — emphasizing timely support after crashes and injuries, not only marketing efficiency.

“Most people are overwhelmed minutes after a collision,” Carr said. “We built systems that respect how injured people actually feel — and that still move quickly when every hour matters for evidence and care.”

Co-Founder Scott Tischler added: “Kathy brings heart into technology. She understands injury recovery from the healthcare side. That changes how we train intake and how we support clients and attorneys.”

**About WreckMatch**

WreckMatch LLC operates a **legal referral service** (not a law firm) connecting car and truck accident victims with licensed attorneys from a network of **800+ participating law firms**. Victims can get matched at **https://www.wreckmatch.com** or call **855 WRECKMATCH · (855) 897-3256**.
